Trans hersteries

A curated exploration of transgender right history.

On the afternoon of the 12 February 2026, the European Parliament voted the Recognition of Trans Woman as Woman.

In a overwhelming context of regression regarding LGTQIA+ right all around the globe, this new resolution from the European Parliament send a indefectible message of hope for the trans community. While the movement of Homosexual Liberation and Trans right was born in America the re-elected Trump administration has been in the past years leading a politic of austerity and discrimination against queer right. Pushing Stonewall and the first Pride marches into the realm of distant memories. This shift question the perennity of this so called acquired rights, and invites us to look back at their history. As theorized by Tuula Juvonen in their work around queer archival, it is complicated to draw a proper timeline of Trans and Queer history because experienced outside of the "formal norms" (understood as mariage, children, etc...) and highly silenced by historians.

This curated exploration is offering a first draft of visualization of the Trans History in a both linear and non linear manner. It is highly based on the analyse of progress in legal, medical and social matter regarding trans right around the globe.
